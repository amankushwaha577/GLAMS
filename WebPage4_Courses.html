GLA University Admission 2020:Entrance Exam, Last Date, Admission form:

Candidates can apply online or offline on payment of INR 700 (INR 500 for B.Ed) as application fee. For UG courses, candidates must have passed 10+2 exam with a minimum aggregate of 50%. For Professional courses, the minimum aggregate of 50% is required in the relevant discipline except for B.Tech and M.Tech which require 60% aggregate. Admission to all the courses is based on performance in GLAET followed by an interview. GLAET is an online MCQs based test of varying duration (60 min to 2 hours) depending on the course applied for. Apart from GLAET, MBA admission is also offered on the basis of a valid score in CAT/ MAT/ XAT or UPSEE. For M.Tech course, GATE score is also acceptable at the time of admission. For Ph.D. (Full-Time) and Part-Time courses, GLA University conducts separate entrance test. CSIR NET/ UGC NET/ GATE or equivalent exam qualified candidates are also eligible to apply.

GLA University B.Tech, B.Pharm & D.Pharm Admission 2020 :

GLA University offers B.Tech and B.Pharm courses of 4-year duration. Admission is based on candidates’ performance in GLAET followed by personal interview. GLA University also offers a diploma course in Pharmacy. This course is of 2-year duration. GLAET is an online MCQs based test of 2 hours duration. Candidates can apply for the same online as well as offline. The registration fee can be paid either online through credit card, debit card or net banking; or offline through cash or demand draft.
